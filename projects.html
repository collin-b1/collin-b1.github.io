BassGPT (RevolutionUC 2025)

Big Mouth Billy Bass modded to take user input and respond using Google Gemini and ElevenLabs text-to-speech. TTS file is processed and movement data is sent to ESP8266 through webserver to fish. Winner of Most Useless Hack category.
